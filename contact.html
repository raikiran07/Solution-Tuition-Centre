Frequently Asked Questions

What are your class timings?

We offer flexible timings to accommodate students' school schedules. Classes are available throughout the day and evening.

Do you provide home tuition?

Yes, we provide both center-based and home tuition services across Guwahati.

What is your fee structure?

Fees vary based on class, subjects, and format (individual/group). Contact us for customized packages.

Do you offer demo classes?

Yes! We offer free demo classes so students can experience our teaching methodology before enrolling.

Which boards do you cater to?

We provide coaching for CBSE, ICSE, and Assam State Board students.

How do you ensure quality teaching?

Our faculty undergoes regular training, and we maintain small batch sizes for personalized attention. We also conduct regular assessments to track progress.
